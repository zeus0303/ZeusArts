About ZEUS ARTS

Since 2018, ZEUS ARTS was established and purposefully designed by the artist Zeus Jalac
to showcase various artworks particularly vector and sketches.
Some of the artworks that was displayed in this webpage were based on the artist's choice of model to portray
and others were based on the customer's request as we value their choice of art design and preference as well.
ZEUS ARTS is open and available for commission for a variety of artwork projects.
